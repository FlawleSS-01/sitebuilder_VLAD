	Gaming Glossary – Key Terms for 6999Bet Players
	New to the world of online casinos? This glossary explains the most common terms you'll find at 6999Bet. Understanding these concepts will help you play smarter and have a more enjoyable gaming experience.
	Bankroll Management	The practice of managing the total amount of money you have set aside for gaming. Smart bankroll management involves setting limits on your bets to ensure you play responsibly and extend your playtime.
	RNG (Random Number Generator)	A technology used in all our non-live casino games to ensure fair and random outcomes. The RNG produces unpredictable results for every spin or card deal, guaranteeing that games cannot be manipulated.
	Return to Player (RTP)	The theoretical percentage of all wagered money a slot machine or casino game will pay back to players over time. Games with a higher RTP generally offer better chances of winning in the long run.
	Payline	A line on a slot machine's reels where winning combinations of symbols must land for a player to win a prize. Modern video slots can have dozens or even hundreds of paylines.
	Live Dealer Games	Casino games that are streamed in real-time with a human dealer managing the action. This format combines the convenience of online play with the authentic atmosphere of a physical casino.
	Progressive Jackpot	A jackpot for a slot game that increases each time the game is played but the jackpot is not won. It continues to grow until one lucky player wins the entire amount, which can be life-changing.
	Free Spins	A type of bonus that allows you to play a set number of rounds on a slot game without using your own money. Winnings from free spins are often subject to wagering requirements.
	Wagering Requirement	A condition applied to bonuses that specifies the amount of money you must bet before you can withdraw any winnings earned from that bonus. It is typically shown as a multiplier, like 30x.
	Odds	In sports betting, odds represent the probability of an event happening and determine how much money you will win if your bet is successful. Higher odds indicate a lower probability but a larger potential payout.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	A standard verification process that we use to confirm a player's identity. Completing the KYC process by submitting documents like an ID is required to ensure account security and process withdrawals.
