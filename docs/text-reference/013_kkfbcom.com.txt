	New to the world of online casinos? Our glossary explains the most common terms you'll encounter at Kkfb, helping you play with more confidence and understanding. Familiarize yourself with these concepts to enhance your gaming experience.
	Bankroll	The total amount of money a player has specifically set aside for gambling. Managing your bankroll is a key part of responsible gaming.
	RTP (Return to Player)	The theoretical percentage of all wagered money that a specific slot machine will pay back to players over time. A higher RTP generally indicates better odds for the player over the long term.
	Free Spins	A type of casino bonus that grants a player a certain number of spins on a particular slot game without using their own money. Winnings from free spins may be subject to wagering requirements.
	Payline	A line on the reels of a slot machine where winning combinations of symbols must land for a payout to be awarded. Modern video slots can have dozens or even hundreds of paylines.
	Live Dealer Games	Casino games, such as Blackjack or Roulette, that are streamed in real-time from a studio with a human dealer. Players can interact with the dealer and other players for an immersive experience.
	Jackpot	A large, special cash prize offered in certain games. A 'progressive jackpot' is one that increases in value every time the game is played but not won.
	Volatility	A term used to describe the level of risk in a slot game. High-volatility slots offer larger but less frequent wins, while low-volatility slots offer smaller but more frequent payouts.
	RNG (Random Number Generator)	A sophisticated computer algorithm that ensures the outcomes of casino games are completely random and fair. It is the technology that guarantees games cannot be rigged.
	Cashback	A promotional offer where the casino refunds a player a certain percentage of their net losses over a specific period. It acts as a safety net and a reward for playing.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	The mandatory process of verifying a player's identity, typically required before making a withdrawal. This security measure helps prevent fraud and ensures a safe gaming environment.
