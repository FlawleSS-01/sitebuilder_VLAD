	Gaming Glossary – Key Casino Terms Explained
	New to the world of online casinos? This glossary will help you understand the most common terms you'll encounter at 999BD. Familiarizing yourself with this terminology will help you play with more confidence and make more informed decisions.
	Wagering Requirement	The total amount of money you must bet before you can withdraw winnings from a bonus. For example, a 10x wagering requirement on a 100 Taka bonus means you must place bets totaling 1,000 Taka.
	RTP (Return to Player)	The percentage of all wagered money a slot machine or casino game will pay back to players over time. Games with a higher RTP theoretically offer better chances of winning in the long run.
	Free Spins	A type of bonus that allows you to play a certain number of rounds on a specific slot game without using your own money. Any winnings from these spins are often added to your bonus balance.
	Payline	A line on a slot machine's reels where winning combinations of symbols must land for you to receive a payout. Modern video slots can have dozens or even hundreds of paylines.
	Bankroll	The total amount of money a player has specifically set aside for gambling. Managing your bankroll responsibly is a key part of safe and enjoyable online gaming.
	RNG (Random Number Generator)	A computer algorithm that produces a sequence of random outcomes for casino games like slots and digital table games. It ensures that every result is fair, independent, and cannot be predicted.
	Live Dealer Game	An online casino game that is played with a real human dealer via a live video stream. This format combines the convenience of online play with the authentic atmosphere of a physical casino.
	Progressive Jackpot	A jackpot for a slot machine that increases each time the game is played but the jackpot is not won. A small portion of each bet contributes to the prize pool, which can grow to enormous amounts.
	Volatility	This refers to the risk level of a slot game. High volatility slots offer larger but less frequent wins, while low volatility slots offer smaller but more frequent payouts.
	Cashback	A promotion where the casino returns a percentage of a player's net losses over a specific period. It acts as a safety net and gives you a second chance to play.
