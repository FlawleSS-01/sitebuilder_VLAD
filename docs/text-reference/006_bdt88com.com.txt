	Betting Glossary – Key Terms for BDT88 Players
	Whether you are new to online gaming or a seasoned player, understanding the terminology is key to a great experience. This glossary from BDT88 explains the most important terms you'll encounter on our platform in simple, clear language.
	RTP (Return to Player)	The percentage of all wagered money a slot machine will pay back to players over time. Games with a higher RTP theoretically offer better chances of winning in the long run.
	Volatility	This refers to the risk level of a slot game. High-volatility slots offer larger but less frequent wins, while low-volatility slots provide smaller but more frequent payouts.
	Wagering Requirement	A condition applied to bonuses that requires you to bet a certain amount of money before you can withdraw your bonus funds and winnings. It's usually expressed as a multiplier (e.g., 20x).
	Live Dealer Games	These are real casino games streamed live to your device from a studio. You can play with and interact with a human dealer and other players for an authentic casino experience.
	Free Spins	A type of bonus that allows you to play a specified number of rounds on a slot game without using your own money. It's a popular way to try new games and win real cash.
	Bankroll Management	The practice of managing the total funds you have set aside for gambling. Smart bankroll management involves setting limits to ensure you play responsibly and sustainably.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	A standard verification process used by licensed platforms like BDT88. It involves submitting identification documents to prove your identity, which helps prevent fraud and secure your account.
	Cash Out	A feature in sports betting that allows you to settle a bet before the event has finished. You can use it to lock in a profit if your bet is winning or to minimize a loss if it looks like it might lose.
	Handicap Betting	A way to make a sporting event more even by giving one team a virtual advantage or disadvantage. The favored team must win by a certain margin for bets on them to succeed.
	Provably Fair	A technology used primarily in crypto casino games that allows players to independently verify that the outcome of a game was random and not manipulated by the platform.
