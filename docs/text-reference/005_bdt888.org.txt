	Casino & Betting Glossary for BDT888 Players
	Welcome to the BDT888 glossary. Here, we explain common terms you'll encounter in online casinos and sports betting. Understanding this vocabulary will help you play with more confidence and make smarter decisions.
	Return to Player (RTP)	The percentage of wagered money a slot game is expected to pay back to players over millions of spins. A higher RTP, such as 96%, indicates a lower house edge and better potential long-term returns.
	Volatility	This describes the risk level of a slot game. High volatility games offer larger but less frequent wins, while low volatility games provide smaller, more consistent payouts, suiting different play styles.
	Payline	A specific line across the reels where winning symbol combinations must land for you to receive a payout. While classic slots had one payline, modern video slots can feature hundreds or even thousands.
	Live Dealer Game	An online game that connects you via a high-definition video stream to a real human dealer managing a physical table. It offers an immersive and authentic casino experience from your own home.
	Welcome Bonus	A promotional offer exclusively for new players who register and make their first deposit. This bonus typically matches your deposit amount with extra funds, giving you more to play with.
	Free Spins	A bonus that grants you a specific number of free rounds on a particular slot game without using your own money. Winnings from free spins are usually credited as bonus funds with wagering requirements.
	Progressive Jackpot	A grand prize in a slot or network of slots that grows with every bet placed by players. It continues to increase until one player wins the full amount, which can often be a life-changing sum.
	Bankroll Management	The essential practice of managing the funds you've set aside for gambling. It involves setting strict win, loss, and deposit limits to ensure you always play responsibly and within your means.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	A mandatory security procedure to verify a player's identity, typically before processing a withdrawal. It requires submitting documents to prevent fraud and protect your account.
	Multiplier	A special feature found in many slot games that multiplies your winnings by a predetermined figure. For instance, a 5x multiplier will increase a winning spin's payout by five times.
