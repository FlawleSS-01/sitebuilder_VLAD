	Gaming Glossary: Key Terms for UuUbd Players
	Welcome to the UuUbd glossary. Understanding these common terms will help you navigate the world of online gaming with more confidence, make smarter decisions, and get the most out of your experience on our platform.
	Return to Player (RTP)	The theoretical percentage of all wagered money a specific slot or game will pay back to players over time. Games with a higher RTP generally offer better long-term winning chances.
	Volatility	Also known as variance, this measures the risk level of a slot game. Low volatility slots pay out small wins frequently, while high volatility slots offer larger, less frequent jackpots.
	Wagering Requirement	A condition applied to bonuses that requires you to bet the bonus amount a certain number of times before you can withdraw any associated winnings. It's a key term for using promotions wisely.
	Provably Fair	An algorithm used primarily in crypto casinos that allows players to independently verify that game results are random and have not been tampered with. It guarantees a transparent and fair gaming process.
	Bankroll Management	The practice of managing the total amount of money you have set aside for gambling. Effective bankroll management involves setting limits to ensure you play responsibly and sustainably.
	Live Betting	Also called in-play betting, this allows you to place wagers on a sports event after it has already started. The odds change in real-time based on the action unfolding in the match.
	Payline	A line on a slot machine's reels where winning combinations must land for you to receive a payout. Modern video slots can have anywhere from one to thousands of paylines.
	Free Spins	A type of bonus that allows you to play a set number of rounds on a specific slot game without using your own money. Winnings from free spins may be subject to wagering requirements.
	Progressive Jackpot	A jackpot that increases in value every time the game is played but the jackpot is not won. It continues to grow until one lucky player wins the entire amount, which can often be life-changing.
	Cryptocurrency Wallet	A digital wallet used to send, receive, and store cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and USDT. You need one to deposit and withdraw funds at a crypto-focused casino like UuUbd.
