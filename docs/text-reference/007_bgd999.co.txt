	Gaming Glossary: Key Terms Explained
	New to online gaming or just want to brush up on the terminology? Our glossary explains the most common terms you'll encounter at Bgd999, helping you play with greater confidence and understanding.
	RTP (Return to Player)	The percentage of all wagered money a slot machine or casino game will pay back to players over time. A higher RTP offers a better chance of winning in the long run.
	Wagering Requirement	A condition applied to bonuses that requires you to bet a certain amount of money before you can withdraw your winnings. It's usually shown as a multiplier, like 30x.
	Free Spins	A type of bonus that allows you to play a set number of rounds on a specific slot game without using your own money. The winnings are often subject to wagering requirements.
	Live Dealer Game	An online casino game that is streamed in real-time with a human dealer. It combines the convenience of online play with the authentic atmosphere of a land-based casino.
	Progressive Jackpot	A jackpot for a slot game that increases each time the game is played but the jackpot is not won. It continues to grow until one lucky player wins the entire amount.
	Payline	A line on a slot machine's reels where winning symbol combinations must land for you to receive a payout. Modern slots can have dozens or even hundreds of paylines.
	Bankroll	The total amount of money a player has specifically set aside for gambling. Managing your bankroll effectively is a key part of responsible gaming.
	Odds	In sports betting, odds represent the probability of an outcome and determine how much money you can win from your bet. They can be shown in decimal, fractional, or American formats.
	Cash Out	A feature in sports betting that allows you to settle a bet before the event has finished. This lets you lock in a profit or minimize a potential loss based on the live action.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	The process of verifying a player's identity, which is a requirement for licensed casinos. It helps prevent fraud and ensures a secure gaming environment for everyone.
