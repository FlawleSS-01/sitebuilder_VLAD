	Casino Glossary – Key Terms for BN88 Players
	New to the world of online casinos? This glossary explains common terms you'll find at BN88, helping you play with more confidence and understanding. Familiarize yourself with these concepts to enhance your gaming experience.
	Return to Player (RTP)	The theoretical percentage of all wagered money that a slot machine will pay back to players over time. A higher RTP offers a better long-term return.
	Volatility	This describes the risk level of a slot game. High-volatility games offer larger but less frequent wins, while low-volatility games provide smaller, more frequent payouts.
	Live Dealer Games	These are real casino games, like Blackjack or Roulette, streamed in high definition from a studio with a human dealer. They allow you to play and interact in real-time for an authentic experience.
	Progressive Jackpot	A jackpot for a slot or video poker game that increases each time the game is played but the jackpot is not won. It can grow to be a massive, life-changing amount.
	Payline	A line in a slot game on which winning symbol combinations must land for you to receive a payout. While classic slots had one payline, modern video slots can have dozens or even hundreds.
	Free Spins	A type of bonus that allows you to spin the reels of a slot game a certain number of times without using your own money. It's a popular feature in welcome offers and promotions.
	Wagering Requirement	The total amount of money you must bet before a bonus and any winnings from it can be withdrawn as cash. It's usually expressed as a multiplier, like 20x the bonus amount.
	Bankroll Management	The practice of managing the funds you have set aside for gambling. It involves setting limits on your bets and losses to ensure you play responsibly and prolong your gameplay.
	Multiplier	A special feature found in many slot games that multiplies your winnings. For example, a 5x multiplier on a win would increase your payout by five times.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	A mandatory verification process used by licensed online casinos to confirm a player's identity. It helps prevent fraud and is often required before you can make a withdrawal.
