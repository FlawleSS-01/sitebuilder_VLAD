	Welcome to the Uubd glossary. Here we explain the most common casino terms in simple, clear language to help you understand the games better and play with more confidence.
	RTP (Return to Player)	The percentage of all wagered money a slot game is expected to pay back to players over a very long period. A higher RTP offers a better theoretical return over millions of spins.
	Volatility	This describes the risk level of a slot game. Low volatility slots pay out small wins frequently, while high volatility slots pay out larger wins but less often.
	Payline	A line on the reels of a slot machine where matching symbols must land to form a winning combination. Modern slots can have anywhere from one to thousands of paylines.
	Wild Symbol	A special symbol in a slot game that can substitute for most other symbols on the reels, helping to create or improve winning combinations, much like a joker in a deck of cards.
	Scatter Symbol	A unique symbol in slots that often triggers bonus features, such as free spins or mini-games. Typically, you only need a certain number of them to appear anywhere on the reels, not necessarily on a payline.
	Free Spins	A popular bonus feature that allows you to spin the reels a set number of times without wagering any of your own money. Any winnings you earn during free spins are added to your balance.
	Bankroll	The total amount of money a player has specifically set aside for gambling. Managing your bankroll responsibly is key to having a safe and enjoyable gaming experience.
