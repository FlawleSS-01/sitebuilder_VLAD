	Navigating the exciting world of online casinos is easier when you understand the language. Our glossary at 3333BD is designed to help new and experienced players alike by clearly defining the most common terms you'll encounter while playing our games and claiming bonuses.
	Wagering Requirement	The number of times you must bet a bonus amount before being able to withdraw any winnings. For example, a 10x wagering requirement on a ৳100 bonus means you must place bets totaling ৳1,000 before cashing out.
	RTP (Return to Player)	The theoretical percentage of all wagered money a specific slot or game will pay back to players over time. Games with a higher RTP, such as 96% or more, generally offer better long-term winning chances.
	Volatility	This describes the risk level of a slot game. High volatility slots offer larger but less frequent wins, while low volatility slots provide smaller but more frequent payouts, catering to different playing styles.
	Live Dealer Games	Casino games that are played with a real human dealer via a live video stream. This format combines the convenience of online play with the authentic atmosphere of a land-based casino.
	Bankroll	The total amount of money a player has specifically set aside for gambling. Managing your bankroll effectively is a key part of responsible gaming, helping you stay in control of your spending.
	Free Spins	A type of casino bonus that allows you to play a set number of rounds on a specific slot game without using your own money. It's a popular way to try out new games and win real money.
	RNG (Random Number Generator)	A sophisticated computer algorithm that ensures the outcomes of casino games (like slot spins or card deals) are completely random and fair. This technology guarantees that results cannot be predicted or manipulated.
	KYC (Know Your Customer)	A standard verification process used by licensed casinos to confirm a player's identity. This usually involves submitting documents to prevent fraud and ensure a secure gaming environment for everyone.
	Payline	A line on a slot machine's reels where winning symbol combinations must land for you to receive a payout. Modern video slots can have anywhere from one to thousands of paylines.
	Cashback Bonus	A promotion where the casino returns a percentage of a player's net losses over a specific period. It acts as a safety net, giving you some of your money back during unlucky streaks.
